Geo Treasure Hunt

How to Play:

Welcome to Geo Treasure Hunt, an exciting game where you explore the real world to uncover clues and reach the hidden treasure!

Objective:

Your mission is to follow a series of clues that lead you to different locations. Each clue will take you to a new spot, and you need to physically visit each location to unlock the next clue.

Steps:

1. Start the Game:

Click on the "Start Game" button to begin your adventure.

You will be shown a Google Street View image of a location.

2. Explore the Location:

Use your knowledge of the world, landmarks, or signs in the image to identify where you are.

The goal is to physically reach the location that matches the snapshot.

3. Reveal the Next Clue:

Once you believe you have reached the correct location, click on the "Reveal Next Clue" button.

Your location will be checked to make sure you are within 100 meters of the target.

If you're too far from the target, you'll need to try again.

4. Repeat the Process:

Continue to follow the clues and explore the world.

Each correct location brings you closer to your final destination and the treasure!
